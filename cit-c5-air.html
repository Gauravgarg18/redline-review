2021 Citroen C5 Aircross Review : 9 Pros & 9 Cons
Pros
  1. A superbly engineered & properly premium crossover
  2. Very stylish exteriors & interiors! We love the design. It's matched to solid build quality
  3. Good quality cabin with comfortable seats, lots of storage & fantastic insulation
  4. 2.0L diesel engine is quick, efficient & extremely refined
  5. Smooth 8-speed AT gearbox impresses
  6. Excellent ride comfort and mature road manners. High speed behaviour is sorted too
  7. Large, well-shaped 580 liter boot gobbles up holiday / airport luggage
  8. Loaded to the gills with features such as a panoramic sunroof, handsfree tailgate opening, customisable instrument cluster, double laminated front windows, terrain modes...
  9. 4-star rating in the Euro NCAP crash test. 3-point seatbelts for all five occupants, 6 airbags, blind spot monitoring system, ESP, hill hold, hill descent control and more
Cons
  1. Rear legroom is strictly adequate. Two 5’10" adults can sit behind each other, but not two 6-footers
  2. No turbo-petrol engine (big miss), no AWD for tourers & no manual gearbox for MT fans
  3. Not really sporty to drive. Cars like the Compass are more fun
  4. Missing some features we expect today (connected car tech, wireless charging, ventilated seats, 360-degree camera, subwoofer…)
  5. The bolstering of the 3 individual rear seats is suitable for slim passengers, not heavier ones
  6. Audio sound quality is alright, but nothing special as you’d expect in a premium car
  7. Many competing crossovers & SUVs offer a 3rd-row of seats, which the C5 Aircross doesn't have
  8. The LHD orientation of many controls (gear shifter, e-brake, bonnet release, engine start button)
  9. Long-term reliability & after-sales service quality are big unknowns
